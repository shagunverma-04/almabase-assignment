NovaMed Systems — Data Processing & Privacy Policy
Version 2.0 | Last Updated: December 2024

1. Data Classification

NovaMed classifies all data processed on behalf of customers into the following tiers:

Tier 1 — Protected Health Information (PHI)
Includes: patient names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, diagnoses, treatment records, insurance information, and any data element that could identify a patient. PHI is subject to full HIPAA protections and is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3) at all times.

Tier 2 — Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
Includes: staff names, email addresses, login credentials of healthcare workers. Stored in isolated schemas with RBAC enforcement.

Tier 3 — Operational Data
Includes: system logs, audit trails, performance metrics. Retained for 7 years per HIPAA requirements.

2. Data Residency

US Customers: All data stored in AWS US-East-1 (N. Virginia) with DR replication to US-West-2 (Oregon).
EU Customers: All data stored exclusively in AWS eu-west-1 (Dublin, Ireland). No EU customer data is ever transferred outside the EEA without explicit written consent.
Customers can request confirmation of data residency in writing at any time.

3. Sub-Processors

NovaMed uses the following categories of sub-processors:

Infrastructure & Hosting:
- Amazon Web Services (AWS) — primary cloud infrastructure
- AWS CloudFront — content delivery

Monitoring & Observability:
- Datadog — application performance monitoring (no PHI transmitted)
- PagerDuty — incident alerting (no PHI transmitted)

Communications:
- SendGrid — transactional email (used for system notifications only, no PHI)

Security:
- Qualys — vulnerability scanning (network-layer only, no data access)
- CrowdStrike — endpoint detection on engineering workstations

A complete and current sub-processor list with data processing roles is maintained at trust.novamed.io and updated within 30 days of any change. Customers are notified of material sub-processor changes via email.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while the customer account is active.
Post-termination: Data retained for 90 days after contract termination, then permanently deleted.
Deletion method: AWS S3 secure deletion with 7-pass overwrite for archival storage; database records purged and vacuumed.
Certificate of Destruction: Provided within 10 business days of confirmed deletion.
Backup purge: Backups containing customer data are fully purged within 120 days of termination.

5. Patient Rights & Data Subject Requests

NovaMed supports customers in fulfilling patient rights under HIPAA and GDPR:
- Right of Access: Patient record exports available in HL7 FHIR R4 format within 30 days
- Right to Rectification: Data corrections processed within 5 business days
- Right to Erasure: Supported where not in conflict with legal retention requirements
- Data Portability: Full dataset export in structured, machine-readable format (JSON/FHIR)

Data subject requests should be submitted by the covered entity (the hospital or clinic) through the NovaMed admin portal or via privacy@novamed.io.

6. Breach Notification Procedure

Step 1: Detection — NovaMed's security monitoring detects anomaly or receives report
Step 2: Internal escalation — Security team notified within 1 hour
Step 3: Investigation — Forensic analysis begins within 2 hours
Step 4: Customer notification — Affected customers notified within 48 hours of breach confirmation with known facts and remediation steps
Step 5: Regulatory notification — HHS Office for Civil Rights notified within 60 days if breach affects 500+ individuals, as required by HIPAA Breach Notification Rule
Step 6: Post-incident report — Full incident report delivered to affected customers within 30 days

7. Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

NovaMed signs a HIPAA-compliant Business Associate Agreement with every customer before platform access is granted. The BAA covers:
- Permitted uses and disclosures of PHI
- NovaMed's obligations to protect PHI
- Breach notification obligations
- Sub-contractor (sub-processor) management
- Term and termination conditions
- PHI return and destruction obligations

BAA templates are available upon request from legal@novamed.io. Custom BAA language can be reviewed by NovaMed's legal team for enterprise contracts.
