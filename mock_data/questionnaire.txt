Vendor Security Assessment Questionnaire
NovaMed Systems — EHR Platform
Prepared by: Hospital Procurement & Compliance Team

1. Is the platform HIPAA compliant? What certifications does NovaMed hold?
2. How is patient data encrypted at rest and in transit?
3. What is your data backup frequency and retention policy?
4. Describe your disaster recovery plan. What are your RPO and RTO targets?
5. What uptime SLA does NovaMed guarantee, and what service credits apply if it is not met?
6. How are user access controls and role-based permissions managed?
7. What authentication mechanisms are supported (e.g., SSO, MFA)?
8. What is your policy on third-party data sharing and sub-processors?
9. How do you handle security incidents and data breaches? What is the notification timeline?
10. Where is patient data physically stored? Which cloud provider do you use?
11. How often are penetration tests and security audits conducted?
12. What is your average response time for critical (P1) support tickets?
13. Can the platform integrate with existing hospital systems via HL7 or FHIR?
14. What is the process for data export and patient record portability upon contract termination?
15. How is NovaMed employee access to customer data governed and monitored?
