NovaMed Systems — Compliance & Certifications Overview
Version 1.4 | Last Updated: January 2025

1. Current Certifications

NovaMed Systems holds the following active certifications and attestations:

SOC 2 Type II
- Issued by: Deloitte & Touche LLP
- Scope: Security, Availability, and Confidentiality Trust Service Criteria
- Audit period: October 2023 – September 2024
- Renewal: Annual. Latest report available under NDA upon request.

ISO 27001:2022
- Issued by: BSI Group
- Certified since: March 2024
- Scope: Information Security Management System covering all NovaMed cloud infrastructure and software development processes
- Renewal: 3-year cycle with annual surveillance audits

HIPAA Attestation
- NovaMed undergoes an independent annual HIPAA Security Rule assessment
- Latest assessment completed: November 2024, conducted by Coalfire Systems
- Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) are signed with all customers prior to platform access
- HITECH Act compliance is included in all BAA terms

HITRUST CSF r2 Certification
- Certification level: Implemented, 1-Year (i1) Validated Assessment
- Issued: July 2024
- HITRUST certification covers NovaMed's EHR platform infrastructure and application layer

PCI DSS (Applicable modules only)
- NovaMed's billing module is PCI DSS Level 2 compliant
- Annual Self-Assessment Questionnaire (SAQ) completed with third-party QSA review

GDPR Compliance
- NovaMed operates a GDPR-compliant data processing framework for EU customers
- EU customer data is stored exclusively in AWS eu-west-1 (Dublin, Ireland)
- Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) are available for all EU customers
- Data Protection Officer (DPO): dpo@novamed.io

4. Regulatory & Legal Compliance

NovaMed's platform is designed to support customer compliance with the following regulations:
- HIPAA / HITECH (US healthcare)
- GDPR (European Union)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- 21 CFR Part 11 (FDA electronic records, for applicable customers)
- ONC Health IT Certification (2015 Edition Cures Update)

NovaMed maintains a dedicated compliance team of 8 specialists and retains external legal counsel specializing in healthcare data privacy law.
